EXPERIENCES

SPORTS

Who says that during holidays you cannot stay in shape? Our guests can relax and stay fit at the same time. A great variety of sports is available at Mendi. You can choose to play tennis, basketball or beach volley. Table tennis, Mini golf or gym is available as well. And don't forget, swimming at the splendid beach is the absolute exercise of summer, combining fun with workout. Last, but not least, people who love to hike, can walk along the green paths next to the property.
Furthermore, our staff will be glad to arrange a bicycle rental, or organized water sports (scuba diving, water ski, windsurfing etc) through our specialized partners.

RELAXATION

Relax by the pool or the beach with a refreshing cocktail from the bar or participate to a yoga class which takes place at the premises from time to time. For your ultimate chill out, book a place at the steam room or the Jacuzzi.

SWIMMING

The large pool at Mendi hotel right next to the beach, surrounded by the garden, will not disappoint you. The sea water will make you feel like you swim at the sea. And the children's pool right next to it, will provide the sense of safety for our younger guests. Lie down at the sun beds around and enjoy a refreshing cocktail or a light snack from the bar.
Furthermore, Mendi beach, one of the most beautiful beaches of the area, awarded with the Blue Flag for the crystal clear turquoise water, is definitely a top choice. Tucked away from traffic and noise, a combination of green and blue, sun and shade, a piece of heaven on earth. Sun beds and umbrellas are available to ensure the comfort of a day on the beach
